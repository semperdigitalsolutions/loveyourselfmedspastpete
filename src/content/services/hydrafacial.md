## Polished, Luminous, Camera-Ready

The HydraFacial is a multi-step facial treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, extracts, and hydrates the skin using patented technology and nourishing serums. ## How It Works

The HydraFacial uses a unique vortex-fusion delivery system that combines three essential steps:

**Cleanse + Exfoliate** — Dead skin cells and surface impurities are gently removed, revealing fresh, healthy skin beneath.

**Extract + Hydrate** — Painless suction removes debris from pores while hydrating serums are simultaneously delivered deep into the skin.

**Fuse + Protect** — The skin is saturated with antioxidants and peptides to maximize your glow and protect against environmental damage.
