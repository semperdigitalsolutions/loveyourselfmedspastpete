After a thorough skin assessment, your treatment begins with gentle numbing for complete comfort. The microneedling device creates precision micro-channels while PRP or exosomes are applied, allowing deep penetration of these powerful regenerative compounds. The session concludes with a soothing mask and post-treatment guidance.

Results develop progressively over 4-6 weeks as new collagen forms. A series of treatments is typically recommended for optimal results.
