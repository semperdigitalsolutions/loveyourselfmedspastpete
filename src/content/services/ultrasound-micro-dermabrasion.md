## Deep Cleanse, Bright Results

*All Advanced Facials include an InstaSculpting modality for enhanced skin tightening and rejuvenation.*

Ultrasound Micro-Dermabrasion uses advanced ultrasonic technology to deeply cleanse, exfoliate, and infuse active ingredients into the skin. This treatment removes dead skin cells, unclogs pores, and enhances product absorption for immediately brighter, smoother skin — all without the harshness of traditional crystal or diamond-tip dermabrasion.

## How It Works

Ultrasonic vibrations gently lift away dead skin cells and impurities while simultaneously driving nutrient-rich serums deep into the skin. The dual-action approach means you get thorough exfoliation and deep nourishment in a single, comfortable treatment.

## Benefits

- **Gentle yet effective** exfoliation without irritation
- **Immediately brighter** and smoother complexion
- **Deep pore cleansing** and unclogging
- **Enhanced absorption** of active skincare ingredients
- **No downtime** — return to your day with a glow
