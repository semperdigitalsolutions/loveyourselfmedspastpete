## Reveal Your Most Luminous Skin

Our Chemical and Organic Peels are designed to deeply exfoliate, refine, and refresh your complexion — removing dull surface cells to reveal the radiant, smooth skin beneath. Whether you prefer a gentle organic approach or a clinical-strength treatment, we customize every peel to your skin's unique needs and goals.

## Treatment Options

### Organic Peels
Gentle yet effective botanical-based peels that harness the power of natural enzymes, fruit acids, and plant extracts to brighten, smooth, and renew the skin without harsh chemicals.

### Clinical Chemical Peels
Medical-grade peels that penetrate deeper for more dramatic results — addressing hyperpigmentation, fine lines, acne scarring, and uneven texture with precision-controlled formulations.

## Benefits You May Experience

- **Improved skin clarity** and brightness
- **Reduced hyperpigmentation** and dark spots
- **Smoother texture** and refined pores
- **Diminished fine lines** and surface irregularities
- **Enhanced product absorption** for better at-home results
- **A natural, lasting glow**

## The Experience

Your peel begins with a thorough skin analysis to select the ideal formulation and strength for your concerns. The solution is applied with precision, and you may feel a gentle tingling as it works to dissolve dull, damaged surface cells. A soothing neutralizer and hydrating mask complete the experience, leaving your skin refreshed and luminous.

Your provider will recommend a treatment schedule and at-home care plan to maintain and build on your results.

Results may vary based on individual physiology, lifestyle, and adherence to treatment plans.
