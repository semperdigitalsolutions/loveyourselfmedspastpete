## Artful Enhancement, Natural Results

Our injectable treatments are designed to softly restore balance, smooth expression lines, and support a naturally refreshed appearance. We combine medical expertise with artistic vision to create subtle, natural-looking results that enhance your features without changing who you are.

Every treatment is performed by our skilled medical professionals who understand facial anatomy, aging patterns, and the art of balanced enhancement. We take a conservative, customized approach—because the best work is the work no one notices.

## Our Injectable Services

### Neuromodulators (Botox, Dysport, Xeomin)
These treatments relax the muscles that cause dynamic wrinkles—crow's feet, forehead lines, and frown lines. The result is a refreshed, relaxed appearance that still allows natural expression.

- Forehead lines
- Crow's feet
- Frown lines (11s)
- Brow lift
- Lip flip
- Gummy smile correction

### Dermal Fillers
Hyaluronic acid fillers restore volume, smooth lines, and enhance facial contours. We use premium brands and precise techniques for beautiful, long-lasting results.

- Cheek enhancement
- Under-eye hollows
- Nasolabial folds (smile lines)
- Marionette lines
- Lip enhancement
- Jawline contouring
- Chin augmentation

### Combination Treatments
Often, the most beautiful results come from a thoughtful combination of neuromodulators and fillers, addressing multiple concerns for comprehensive facial rejuvenation.

## What to Expect

Most injectable treatments take 15-45 minutes, depending on the areas treated. Some results are immediate (fillers), while others develop over 1-2 weeks (neuromodulators).

You may experience mild swelling, redness, or bruising at injection sites—these typically resolve within a few days. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to optimize your results.

## Results Timeline

- **Neuromodulators:** Results appear in 3-7 days, full effect at 2 weeks, lasting 3-4 months
- **Fillers:** Immediate results with final appearance after swelling subsides (1-2 weeks), lasting 6-18 months depending on product and area
