## Reveal Fresh, Radiant Skin

*All Advanced Facials include an InstaSculpting modality for enhanced skin tightening and rejuvenation.*

Our Micro-Peel treatments use medical-grade, organic and clean formulations to resurface, brighten, and renew the skin. Choose from our 30% Glycolic Peel or Collagen Pro Peel — both designed to remove damaged outer layers and reveal the fresh, radiant skin beneath.

## Peel Options

### 30% Glycolic Peel
A powerful alpha-hydroxy acid peel that exfoliates the outer layers of skin, stimulating cell turnover and revealing a brighter, smoother complexion. Effective for dullness, uneven tone, fine lines, and mild acne scarring.

### Collagen Pro Peel
A specialized peel formulated to boost collagen production while gently resurfacing the skin. This treatment targets fine lines, loss of firmness, and overall skin aging for a more youthful, plump appearance.

*Both formulations are organic and clean — free from harsh chemicals and synthetic additives.*

## Benefits

- **Brighter, more even** skin tone

## The Experience

Your Micro-Peel begins with a skin consultation to determine the best peel formulation for your needs. After a gentle cleanse, the peel solution is carefully applied and monitored. A soothing mask and hydrating finish complete the treatment, leaving your skin refreshed and luminous.

Some mild peeling may occur in the days following treatment as your skin renews itself. Your provider will give you detailed aftercare instructions.

Results may vary. A consultation is recommended to choose the right peel for your skin type.
