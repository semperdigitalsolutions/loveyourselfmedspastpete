## Precision Rejuvenation for Delicate Areas

*All Advanced Facials include an InstaSculpting modality for enhanced skin tightening and rejuvenation.*

Micro-Stamping is a gentler alternative to traditional micro-needling, delivering targeted treatment to specific areas of concern with precision and control. This technique is ideal for delicate areas like under-eyes, lips, and areas requiring focused rejuvenation where traditional micro-needling may be too aggressive.

## How It Works

Using a specialized stamping device, your provider creates controlled micro-channels in targeted areas, stimulating collagen production and enhancing the absorption of active serums. The stamping motion provides more precise depth control than rolling techniques, making it safer and more effective for sensitive areas.

## Benefits

- **Targeted treatment** for delicate facial areas
- **Gentler approach** with precise depth control
- **Stimulates collagen** production in focused zones
- **Enhances product absorption** for maximum efficacy
- **Minimal downtime** and comfortable treatment experience
- **Ideal complement** to other facial treatments

## Treatment Areas

- Under-eye hollows and fine lines
- Lip lines and lip area
- Acne scars and hyperpigmentation spots
- Crow's feet and forehead lines
- Any area requiring focused, delicate treatment

Results may vary. A consultation is recommended to determine the best treatment plan for your skin.
