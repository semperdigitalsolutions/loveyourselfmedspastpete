## Lashes That Speak for Themselves

Lux Lashes delivers premium lash extension services for fuller, longer, and more dramatic lashes. Whether you prefer a natural, everyday look or full glamour, our skilled lash artists create a custom set tailored to your eye shape, lifestyle, and aesthetic preferences.

## Our Lash Services

- **Classic Lash Extensions** — one extension per natural lash for a natural, polished look
- **Volume Lash Extensions** — multiple lightweight extensions per lash for dramatic fullness
- **Hybrid Sets** — a blend of classic and volume for customized dimension
- **Lash Fills** — maintenance appointments to keep your set looking fresh
- **Lash Lifts & Tints** — enhance your natural lashes with a semi-permanent curl and color

## The Experience

Your lash appointment begins with a consultation to discuss your desired look. You'll then relax comfortably while our lash artist meticulously applies each extension for a beautiful, long-lasting result.
