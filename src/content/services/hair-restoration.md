## Our Hair Restoration Treatments

### Microneedling with Exosomes for Hair Growth
The foundation of our hair restoration program. Precision microneedling delivers powerful exosome therapy directly to the scalp, stimulating dormant follicles, promoting new growth, and strengthening existing hair at the cellular level. Exosomes deliver growth factors and signaling molecules that activate the body's natural hair regeneration processes.

### Red Light Therapy
Low-level red light therapy stimulates cellular energy production in hair follicles, increasing circulation and promoting hair growth. This painless, non-invasive treatment supports follicle health and works synergistically with our microneedling protocols.

### Head Spa Treatment
Our luxurious Head Spa experience combines deep scalp cleansing, therapeutic massage, and customized treatments to create the optimal scalp environment for hair growth. This relaxing treatment removes buildup, improves circulation, and nourishes the scalp.
