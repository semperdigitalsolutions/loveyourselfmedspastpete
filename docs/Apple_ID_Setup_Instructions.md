# Setting Up Your New Apple ID for Love Yourself Med Spa

**New Apple ID email:** shari@loveyourselfmedspastpete.com

These instructions will walk you through creating a new Apple Account and signing into your iPad with it. This keeps your personal Apple ID separate from the business.

---

## Step 1: Create the Apple ID on the iPad

1. Open **Settings** on the iPad
2. Tap your name at the top (or "Sign in to your iPad" if not signed in)
3. If you're currently signed in with a personal Apple ID, scroll down and tap **Sign Out** first
4. Tap **"Don't have an Apple Account?"** or **"Create a Free Apple Account"**
5. Enter your **birthday** and **name**, then tap Continue
6. When asked for an email, enter: **shari@loveyourselfmedspastpete.com**
7. Create a strong password (at least 8 characters, with a number, uppercase, and lowercase letter)
8. Set your **country/region** to United States
9. Set up **two-factor authentication** — use your personal phone number to receive verification codes
10. Apple will send a verification email to shari@loveyourselfmedspastpete.com — open that email and tap the verification link

## Step 2: If you're already signed in with another Apple ID

If the iPad is already set up with your personal Apple ID:

1. Go to **Settings** > tap your name at the top
2. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Sign Out**
3. You may be asked to keep a copy of your data on the iPad — choose what you'd like
4. Enter your personal Apple ID password to turn off Find My iPad
5. Once signed out, tap **Sign in to your iPad**
6. Tap **"Don't have an Apple Account?"** and follow Step 1 above

## Step 3: Alternative — Create via Web Browser

You can also create the Apple ID at: https://appleid.apple.com/account

1. Go to https://appleid.apple.com/account
2. Fill in your name, birthday, and use **shari@loveyourselfmedspastpete.com** as the email
3. Create your password and set up security questions
4. Verify the email address
5. Then sign into the iPad with the new Apple ID from **Settings**

## Important Notes

- Keep your password somewhere safe (a password manager is recommended)
- This Apple ID can be used for the App Store, iCloud, and any business apps on the iPad
- No charges will occur unless you make a purchase
- For more details, see Apple's official guide: https://support.apple.com/en-us/108647
