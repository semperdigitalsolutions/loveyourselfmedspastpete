# Love Yourself Med Spa — 20% Off Landing Page Design Spec

## Core purpose
Create a focused Google Ads landing page for a new-client offer: **20% off your first treatment**.

## Visual direction
- Luxury med spa aesthetic
- Cream, blush, rose-gold, muted cocoa, and soft gold accents
- Serif display headlines with clean sans-serif body copy
- Lots of white space, soft cards, thin borders, elegant dividers

## Palette
| Token | Hex |
|---|---|

| Gold | `#d4a75e` |

## Fonts
Recommended web fonts:
- Headings: `Cormorant Garamond`
- Body/navigation/buttons: `Montserrat`

## Page sections
1. Header with logo, nav, and Book Now button
2. Hero with 20% offer, short value statement, Book Consultation and Call Now CTAs
3. Trust strip: Physician-Guided Care, Personalized Plans, Luxury Experience, Tierra Verde Location
4. Signature services cards: HydraFacial, Microneedling, Body Contouring, Chemical Peels, Injectables
5. Why choose section with four trust points and a testimonial/expectations card
6. Limited-time offer CTA band
7. Contact / lead capture section
8. Footer with phone, website, address, and quick links

## Important production notes
- Replace placeholder service card images with real approved spa images when available.
- Replace the testimonial/expectations card with a verified client review only if you have permission.
- Confirm the offer terms before publishing: new-client eligibility, exclusions, expiration date, and whether it can be combined with other offers.
- Connect the lead form to Mangomint, a CRM, Netlify Forms, HubSpot, or the client’s preferred booking/contact workflow.
- Add Google Ads conversion tracking to Book, Call, and form submission actions.
